VR Corona Apocalypse

 

For this project, I created a Virtual Reality shooter game in first-person using the Oculus Quest 2. The theme of the game is the Coronavirus. This game is set in a zombie apocalypse type of scene, with derelict houses, and people walking around who have been infected by the virus. In this game the player has a mask shooter in which they must shoot masks at the infected people and also avoid making contact with them by keeping socially distant.
