Robot Arm

 

I created an animation of a robot arm pouring tea from a teapot and milk from a milk carton into a cup. I designed the robot arm, the table, teapot, milk carton and cup as well as the floor and background. I animated the robot arm to grab and lift the teapot, rotate and pour tea into the cup, then put the teapot back down. I did the same for the milk carton. I used various cameras at different angles to capture the rendered outputs, which I then used to make the video.
